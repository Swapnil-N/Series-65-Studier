## Physical-device verification (manual reviewer guide)

Once the action-required items above are done:

1. **Deploy.** Merge to `main`. Confirm the GH Action is green. Note the HTTPS URL.
2. **iPhone install.** Open Safari → URL → Share → **Add to Home Screen** → confirm icon + name. Launch from home screen; should be fullscreen (no Safari chrome).
3. **Safe-area.** On a notched device, verify the tab bar is not clipped by the home indicator and the status bar is not overlapping content.
4. **Airplane mode.** Enable airplane mode → relaunch app. All routes load, content renders, quiz can be completed.
5. **Full mock.** Complete a full 130-Q mock offline. Verify: pass/fail shown vs 72.5%, per-topic breakdown, streak incremented, readiness chart updates after first mock.
6. **Force-quit recovery.** Mid-mock, kill Safari from the app switcher, relaunch from the home-screen icon. The mock must resume at the same question with correct remaining time (±2s).
7. **Background > 10 min.** Mid-mock, background the app for 11 minutes, return. Modal should offer Resume/Abandon.
8. **7-day eviction test.** Do not open the app for 7+ days. Reopen offline. Content must still load (bundled in JS); user state (streak, card schedule) may have been evicted — Settings should show a storage warning banner since last export > 5 days. This is documented expected behavior.
9. **Accessibility.** Enable VoiceOver, complete a quiz question. Increase iOS Dynamic Type to XXL — layout should still be readable.
